About

Process Behaviour Charts (XmR) built for NHS healthcare improvement teams. Follows Donald Wheeler's methodology for understanding process variation — distinguishing common cause from special cause.

Part of the Gemba Suite

Free tools for lean methodology at the gemba. gembasuite.org

Methodology & Constants

XmR constants: 2.66 = 3/d₂ (genuine 3σ limits), 3.268 = D₄ for span-2 mR. No patient-identifiable data collected. Process improvement tool only.
