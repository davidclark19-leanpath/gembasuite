Guides & Learning Resources

Everything you need to learn lean methodology and use the Gemba Suite tools — from a first introduction to lean thinking to complete user guides for each app.

These guides are free to use and share under Creative Commons CC BY-NC-SA 4.0. They are written for healthcare improvement teams — ward managers, laboratory leads, biomedical scientists, service improvement practitioners, and clinical staff. No prior lean experience is required for the introductory guides.
